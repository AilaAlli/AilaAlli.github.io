A few ways of how climate change impacts our planet

Sea Acidification

Sea Acidification is the reduction of the oceans ph level. This cn completely change an ecosystem, thus destroying much sea life.

Rising Temperatures

We have nothing to thank but climate change for the rise of our planet's temperatures. In fact, Global temperature hasn't been cooler than the twentieth-century average since 1976. Additionally, 9 out of 10 of the warmest years on record have occurred since 2005

The Melting Polar Ice Caps

Due to an increase of global temperatures, the polar ice caps, an important factor to sea life, are rapidly melting. Besides being important for marine life, ice caps help regulate sea level and global temperatures.
